Frequently Asked Questions

What makes OH Dibs the essential tool for modern brokerages?

OH Dibs is a software platform designed for real estate brokerages to streamline the management of open houses and showing requests, allow agents in your office to post and claim opportunities.

How does OH Dibs ensure optimal agent-to-opportunity matching?

Our system allows listing agents and top agents in your office to post opportunities for hosting open houses or showing property with available and suitable open house hosts and showing agents within your brokerage, ensuring optimal coverage and efficiency for every event.

Are there flexible pricing options, and what are the initial costs?

Yes, there is a one-time setup fee of $500 for the Basic, Standard, and Pro plans. For the Enterprise plan, the setup fee is custom and starts from $500+.

What core features are included across all OH Dibs plans?

All our plans include core features such as Unlimited Open Houses, Effortless Scheduling, Automated Notifications, Mobile-Friendly Interface, Priority Support, and Leverage for Top Agents. Higher-tier plans offer additional benefits like Dedicated Onboarding and Custom Integrations.

Can our Lender pay for our brokerage's subscription?

Yes, your lender can pay for your brokerage's OH Dibs subscription. This can be a great way to leverage partnerships and provide value to your agents.

What level of support can your agents expect from OH Dibs?

We provide priority support for all our plans, ensuring that agents have access to assistance when they need it. This includes email support and access to a comprehensive knowledge base. Pro and Enterprise plans may include dedicated onboarding and account management.

How does OH Dibs safeguard your brokerage's sensitive data?

Data security is a top priority for OH Dibs. We employ industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your brokerage's sensitive information and client data. Regular security audits are conducted to ensure the integrity and confidentiality of your data.
